Chicken Wraps

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts
  • 1 Large Tortilla
  • Mayonnaise (Or any sauce)
  • 2 Slices of Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • salt and pepper

About

I used to eat this chicken wrap all the time when I needed something quick, tasty, and packed with protein. It’s simple to make, but it always hit the spot. With a solid serving of chicken, healthy fats, and some fresh veggies, it gave me just the right balance of nutrients without overcomplicating things. I’d throw it together in minutes, and it always kept me full and satisfied. Whether I was rushing between classes or finishing up a workout, this wrap became my go-to. Easy to eat on the move and always filling—honestly, it made hitting my daily caloric goals way easier.

Macros

  • Calories: 600
  • Protein: 35g
  • Carbohydrates: 26g
  • Fats: 11g

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken breast, then slice it thinly.
  2. Lay the tortilla flat and spread mayonnaise (or your favorite sauce) in the center.
  3. Place lettuce and tomato slices on the tortilla.
  4. Add the sliced chicken on top of the veggies.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Fold the sides of the tortilla and roll it up tightly.
  7. Voila!
