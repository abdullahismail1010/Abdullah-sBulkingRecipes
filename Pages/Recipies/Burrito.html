Burrito

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Flour Tortilla
  • 250g Steak
  • 1/2 Avocado
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup cooked rice (White)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Sour cream
  • Hot Sauce

About

As someone who’s been focused on bulking, these tacos were a go-to for me. Packed with protein from the beef and loaded with healthy fats, they’re a great way to hit my calorie goals without sacrificing flavor. They’re quick to make, customizable, and perfect for fueling muscle growth after a tough workout. Simple, satisfying, and effective.

Macros

  • Calories: 800
  • Protein: 32g
  • Carbohydrates: 25g
  • Fats: 13g

Steps

  1. Season the steak with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat and cook the steak for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until it's cooked to your liking. Let it rest, then slice it into thin strips.
  2. Warm the flour tortilla in the same pan for about 20-30 seconds, just to make it soft and pliable.
  3. Place the cooked rice in the center of the tortilla.
  4. Add the sliced steak on top.
  5. Add the sliced avocado, shredded cheese, and a couple of spoonfuls of sour cream.
  6. Wrap it up: Fold in the sides of the tortilla and roll it up tightly.
  7. Slice in half and enjoy your high-calorie burrito!
  8. Voila!
