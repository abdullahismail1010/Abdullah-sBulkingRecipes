Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1kg Beef Mince
  • 1 Taco Seasoning
  • 8 Taco Shells
  • Shredded cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Salsa
  • Sour cream
  • Hot Sauce

About

As someone who’s been focused on bulking, these tacos were a go-to for me. Packed with protein from the beef and loaded with healthy fats, they’re a great way to hit my calorie goals without sacrificing flavor. They’re quick to make, customizable, and perfect for fueling muscle growth after a tough workout. Simple, satisfying, and effective.

Macros

  • Calories: 700
  • Protein: 33g
  • Carbohydrates: 27g
  • Fats: 12g

Steps

  1. Season the steak with salt and pepper
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat
  3. Cook the steak for 4-5 minutes on each side, or until desired doneness
  4. Let the steak rest for a few minutes, then slice it into thin strips
  5. Warm the tortillas in the same pan for 20-30 seconds, just until soft
  6. Place a spoonful of cooked rice in the center of each tortilla
  7. Add the sliced steak on top
  8. Add slices of avocado, a sprinkle of shredded cheddar cheese, and a spoonful of sour cream
  9. Drizzle with hot sauce if desired
  10. Fold the tortillas and enjoy your delicious steak tacos!
  11. Voila!
