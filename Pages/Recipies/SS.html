Special Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices thick white bread
  • 4 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1-2 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon butter

The combination of creamy peanut butter, rich cheese, and a touch of honey gave me the perfect balance of flavors, and it was so easy to prepare. Steps

  1. Take one slice of bread and spread a generous 4 tablespoons of peanut butter on it. The more peanut butter, the higher the calorie count, so go big!
  2. Place 1-2 slices of cheddar cheese on top of the peanut butter. You can use any cheese you prefer; a rich cheese like cheddar or Swiss will add more calories and flavor.
  3. Heat the taco shells according to the package instructions (usually 5-7 minutes in the oven for crispy, or microwave for soft).
  4. Place the second slice of bread on top, jam side down (if using jam), to complete the sandwich.
  5. Slice the sandwich in half and enjoy! It's a rich, calorie-dense sandwich that's perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.
  6. Voila!
