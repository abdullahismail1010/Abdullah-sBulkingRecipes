High Calorie Beef Mince Pasta

Ingredients

  • 500g Beef Mince
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • 500g Pasta
  • 500ml Water
  • Tumeric
  • Red Chilli
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Black Pepper

About

This is a very straightforward and simple recipe, yet it gets the job done. Beef Mince, especially fatty cuts, has a lot of calories and is also high in protein. Beef also has a lot of micronutrients, adding to its benefits outside of just muscle and fitness. Coupled with pasta, this is a delicious and healthy treat. This recipe is for two servings and can be stored in the fridge for use later.

Macros

  • Calories: 700 cal
  • Protein: 63g
  • Carbohydrates: 54g
  • Fats: 12g

Steps

  1. Season the beef with all the seasonings, leaving it for around 4 hours in a pot (the longer, the better).
  2. Boil your pasta
  3. In the meantime start cooking the beef mince over medium heat for 20 minutes
  4. Add in your pasta
  5. Add in your cheese
  6. Voila!
