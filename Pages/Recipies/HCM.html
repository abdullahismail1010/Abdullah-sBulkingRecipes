High Calorie Milkshake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 250mg Oats
  • Protein Powder (Optional)
  • 250ml Milk
  • 2 TB Peanut Butter
  • Honey
  • Banana

About

This milkshake sure helped me gain weight, with over 1000 calories per serving. With good amounts of protein and fats, it really helped me put on some weight. It is also very simple to make, and easy to gulp down, adding to its effectiveness.

Macros

  • Calories: 1000
  • Protein: 74g
  • Carbohydrates: 24g
  • Fats: 22g

Steps

  1. In your blender, add oats and turn on the blender for 10 seconds (this will create a smooth and delicious consistency).
  2. Add the milk, peanut butter, honey, and optional protein powder.
  3. Blend for 2 minutes.
  4. Voila!
