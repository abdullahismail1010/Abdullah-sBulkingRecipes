Bagel and Five Eggs

Ingredients

  • Bagel
  • 5 Eggs

About

Sure this recipe might not be fancy, but it sure as hell gets the job done. Eggs are considered one of the best proteins, due to their digestibility and also super high in micronutrients paired with a high carb source. This recipe or ingredients are perfect for your muscle building journey.

Macros

  • Calories: 600 cal
  • Protein: 35g
  • Carbohydrates: 42g
  • Fats: 15g

Steps

  1. Cook your eggs whichever way you like them best, fried, boiled, poached etc.
  2. Put your bagels and eggs on a plate and enjoy
  3. Voila!
